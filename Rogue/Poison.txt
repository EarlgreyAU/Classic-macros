This lets you apply all poisons with one macro, by using ALT, CTRL and left/right mouse button

#showtooltip
/use [nomodifier] Instant Poison; [modifier:alt] Crippling Poison II; [modifier:ctrl] Mind-numbing Poison III
/use [button:1] 16; [button:2] 17



/use [button:1] Instant Poison VI; [button:2] Instant Poison VI; [button:3] Instant Poison VI
/use [button:1] 16; [button:2] 17; [button:3] 18
/click StaticPopup1Button1
