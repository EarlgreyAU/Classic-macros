This will do is instantly target the quest mob unless they’re dead, then it will cause you to auto-attack. 
Make sure if you’re using a slow weapon to un-equip it and use your fists as they’re faster. Useful for warriors and casters.

/tar insert name here
/cleartarget [dead]
/startattack

e.g.
/tar Flattusk the Bold




The next macro is for if your class learns an instant cast spell, like moonfire or fireblast. 
Warlocks take note that your dots, unless they do damage upon application, don’t tag the mob for you.



#showtooltip
/tar insert name here
/cleartarget [dead]
/cast spell name here

e.g.
/cast Moonfire
